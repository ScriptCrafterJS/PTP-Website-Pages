What began as a spark of creativity in 2023 has evolved into a thriving global network of innovation. The Palestine Diaspora Technology Network (PDTN), an initiative by Palestine TechnoPark, bridges global Palestinian talent with Palestine's growing tech ecosystem. By leveraging expertise and resources worldwide, PDTN fosters mentorship, career growth, and innovation, helping Palestinian tech professionals unlock new opportunities. Amid adversity, Palestinians have shown resilience as a force for progress. Following the war in Gaza, PDTN emerged to provide Palestinians, wherever they are, with access to support and connections from the diaspora community.

Our Vision

Our vision is to establish a resilient and dynamic global network of Palestinians, united in their dedication to fostering the sustainable development and progress of Palestine. This network transcends geographical boundaries, connecting Palestinians worldwide to collaborate, innovate, and attract investment, all driven by a shared sense of pride, solidarity, and responsibility. By leveraging diverse skills, expertise, and resources, the network aims to drive positive change, nurture collaboration among diaspora members, empower them to contribute their unique talents, and facilitate meaningful partnerships with stakeholders within and outside Palestine. Ultimately, this unified network serves as a catalyst for innovation, entrepreneurship, and knowledge sharing, propelling the growth and prosperity of Palestine's economy.

Our Goals

Building a Members' Database

The network creates a comprehensive list of Palestinian diaspora members, capturing their skills and expertise to facilitate collaboration and strengthen ties with Palestine.

Skill Development

By offering peer mentorship, career guidance, and access to workshops, training, and resources, the network enhances the technical capabilities of Palestinian tech professionals.

Remote Internship Opportunities

The program connects students with remote internships, providing hands-on experience to improve employability and prepare them for real-world tech challenges.

Empowering Human Capital

Through mentoring and resource-sharing, the network fosters continuous learning, innovation, and digital transformation, advancing the tech sector in Palestine.

Our Initiatives Include

  • An online innovation portal showcasing Palestinian startups and enabling partnerships
  • Virtual hackathons and idea camps addressing challenges in sectors like health, education, and environment
  • Online mentorship programs led by industry experts
  • Virtual B2B conferences linking Palestinian businesses with potential clients, suppliers, and investors
  • Partnerships with accelerators/incubators abroad for mentoring and market access support

Palestine Diaspora ICT Network Around the World

Saudi Arabia - LEAP (March 2024)
Palestine TechnoPark showcased innovative technologies and fostered collaboration at the LEAP conference, a premier global tech event.
London - Tech Week (June 2024)
TechnoPark participated with a Palestinian delegation, hosting a networking event at the Mosaic Rooms. The event engaged Palestinian IT professionals in the UK and introduced the "Tech Circles" program, which mentors recent graduates and connects them to global opportunities.
Washington, DC (August 2024)
In collaboration with PaltechUS, TechnoPark hosted a networking event to support Palestine's ICT sector, bringing together diaspora professionals to discuss collaboration, investment, and innovation opportunities.
Dubai - GITEX (October 2024)
Partnering with Intersect Hub and Wamda, TechnoPark organized a networking event for diaspora professionals and entrepreneurs. This event emphasized leveraging diaspora expertise to drive innovation, strengthen infrastructure, and advance Palestine's tech ecosystem.
