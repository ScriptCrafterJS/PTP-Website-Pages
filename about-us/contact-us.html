Conveniently located in Birzeit, Palestine Techno Park is accessible within 15 minutes by car and 30 minutes by public transportation from the central bus station in Ramallah. The Park is also a few minutes away from the Birzeit University Academic Campus and the Palestinian Museum. Palestine Techno Park’s strategic location and proximity to Birzeit University makes the park a metropolis and a national tech hub for your entrepreneurship goals.
