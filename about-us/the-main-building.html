Palestine India Techno Park
Flagship Building

Strategically located within the wider Master Plan, our flagship building stands as a testament to innovative design and a commitment to fostering connected communities. Spanning 7 floors with a total built-up area of 9,500m², the building embodies distinctive elements that invite collaboration and embrace natural daylight.

B

Basement Floor – Don't Just Work, Live Life!

The basement floor caters to holistic well-being, featuring parking spaces, storage, and a health center. This health haven comprises an indoor swimming pool, a relaxation area, a fully equipped gym, sauna, lounge, and a poolside bar, all designed to offer a rejuvenating experience.

G

Ground Floor – Be Part of a Community!

This floor is dedicated to a collaborative community atmosphere. Offering flexible membership plans for freelancers, professionals, and remote workers, our co-working zones can accommodate up to 45 individuals. Alongside office desk spaces, members benefit from shared facilities like phone booths, meeting rooms, library, auditorium, and a seminar room. Gourmet food is served in the cafeteria, fostering a community hub that can accommodate up to 60 people.

1-2

First & Second Floors – Clustering Start-Ups for Creative Collaboration

These floors provide flexible shared office spaces for startups at different growth stages. With 24/7 accessibility, the building offers smart amenities and a professional environment that fosters engagement. The goal is to inspire curiosity, encourage innovation, stimulate collaboration, and increase productivity.

3

Third Floor – Leapfrog Your Business with Innovation!

Dedicated to innovation, the third floor hosts emerging technology labs in Blockchain, FinTech, Virtual Reality, Augmented Reality, Internet of Things, and Robotics. These labs facilitate the adoption of emerging technologies, commercializing new products, and integration with global value chains. The Innovation Labs aim to create a supportive ecosystem through collaborative initiatives involving students, researchers, entrepreneurs, mentors, investors, and private sector companies.

4-5

Fourth & Fifth Floors – Reserve Your Corporate Open Office Space NOW!

The upper floors offer flexible open office spaces with an average area of 120m², featuring exceptional views. Corporate offices provide a flexible interior design allowing for office expansion, aligning with business growth. Premium services and amenities are tailored to corporate needs. The fifth floor additionally features a garden terrace, providing a daily interaction with nature for relaxation or socializing with fellow members.

A Dynamic Ecosystem

At the Palestine India Techno Park, our flagship building is more than just a physical space—it's a dynamic ecosystem designed to inspire, innovate, and cultivate collaboration.
