Governance Structure

Our leadership framework designed to drive innovation and excellence at Palestine Techno Park

Governance Overview

The Palestine Techno Park (PTP) operates under a non-profit organizational framework, strategically guided by a diverse Board of Directors. This collaborative governance model brings together academia and industry to create a powerful synergy that drives our mission forward.

Birzeit University

As a leading educational institution, Birzeit University plays a fundamental role in shaping the strategic direction of Palestine Techno Park. The university's representatives on the Board contribute academic expertise, ensuring our initiatives align with educational objectives and foster collaboration between academia and industry.

Tech Forum

The Tech Forum, established by IT pioneers from the private sector, brings a business-oriented perspective to PTP's governance. Its representatives contribute insights and strategies rooted in private sector experiences, focusing on empowering youth and advancing technological innovation in Palestine.

The collaboration between Birzeit University and the Tech Forum represents a powerful synergy between academia and industry—an essential alliance in pursuing PTP's mission to support Palestine's entrepreneurship ecosystem. Our non-profit structure underscores our commitment to community development, technological advancement, and regional innovation.
