Founded on the premise of creating a collaborative, interactive, and flexible business environment, Palestine Techno Park is an engine of growth and clustering in the technology sector.

Our Story

The Palestine Techno Park (PTP), established in 2016, stands as a research and innovation technology park registered as a non-profit organization...

It emerged from a partnership between Birzeit University and the Tech Forum, with support from the Government of India...

Inaugurated in 2016 by H.E. President Mahmoud Abbas and H.E. Mr. M.J Akbar, the Park is a cornerstone of Palestine's innovation ecosystem.

Vision

To serve as the driving force behind growth and clustering within the technology sector, inspiring innovation and collaboration for a brighter future.

Mission

To establish ourselves as a national hub for technology and innovation, empowering Palestinian youth, women, and entrepreneurs to create impactful solutions and thrive in a global digital economy.

"PTP is home to innovation labs, research hubs, and programs empowering thousands of Palestinian entrepreneurs."
