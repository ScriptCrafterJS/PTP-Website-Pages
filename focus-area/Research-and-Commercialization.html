Research and Commercialization

Advancing innovation and commercialization by supporting the research community in translating discoveries into market-ready technologies

R Research Support Office

The Research Support Office provides a one-stop gateway for partners seeking expertise, infrastructure and linkages to scale their work. We offer executive guidance, technical project management, and administrative assistance to onsite R&D units.

Through our programs and facilities, partners gain access to mentorship from industry veterans, networking events, and a collaborative community of scientists and entrepreneurs.

Q Rising Research Quality

Palestinian research has considerably strengthened in quality, rigour and impact in recent years. By supporting applied R&D and enterprises around ideas with market-fit, we help transition groundbreaking scientific work into tangible benefit for communities and job opportunities.

S Fostering Spinouts and Entrepreneurship

We consider spinouts a key path for realizing economic returns on national R&D spending. Our programs actively promote and mentor spinouts from partner institutions with commercially oriented research. The goal is to maximize innovation through new ventures tackling important issues.

Our Strategic Objectives

1
Facilitating multi-stakeholder partnerships between academia, industry and investors through forums, workshops and networking platforms
2
Identifying commercially promising research through a stringent evaluation process and advising on go-to-market strategies
3
Running accelerator programs to help translate ideas into minimally viable products and seed-funded startups
4
Attracting high-impact local and foreign investment in ventures with innovative solutions and scalable business models
5
Monitoring performance indicators and societal reach of commercialized research
